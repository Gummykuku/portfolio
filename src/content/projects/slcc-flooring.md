## Overview

SLCC Flooring is a regional flooring retailer whose website was losing potential customers at every stage of the funnel. The site had outdated design, confusing navigation, and no clear path from browsing to booking a consultation.

## Problem

Analytics showed a high bounce rate on product pages and almost no traffic reaching the contact form. Users couldn't easily filter by room type, material, or budget — the three questions every flooring customer starts with.

## Process

Heuristic evaluation identified 14 usability issues. I followed up with five customer interviews to understand how people actually shop for flooring — most start with a room in mind, not a material.

I restructured the information architecture around the customer's mental model: **by room first, material second, budget always visible**. The new design introduced a simplified nav, persistent quote CTA, and streamlined product filters.

## Outcome

- Conversion rate increased by **20%**
- Time to reach contact form reduced by half
- Bounce rate on product pages dropped from 68% to 41%
